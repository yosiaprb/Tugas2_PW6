About Rhythmic Echo

Rhythmic Echo is a website that deeply explores the world of rhythm in music. The site is dedicated to exploring aspects of rhythm in various genres of music, from funk, to jazz, to electronic.

Rhythmic Echo provides a comprehensive guide to rhythm, percussion and repetition patterns in music, helping visitors to understand how rhythm affects pitch and melody.
